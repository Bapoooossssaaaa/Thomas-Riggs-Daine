Personal Background

Legal Name: Thomas Michael Daine  |  Date of Birth: April 14, 1998

Discovered Backstory

Thomas Daine revealed more about his background after Operation OP-S66-24-0822. Thomas revealed that prior to the Chaos Insurgency he was a Specialist in the United States Army. More particularly, he served as an 11B in the 2nd Battalion of the 87th Infantry Regiment. During this time, he operated as an Automatic Rifleman providing fire support for his squadmates. During one of his deployments, he was sent to capture a group of terrorists operating in Arizona known as Красный Октябрь (Red October) who were plotting to bomb a large area of the city of Phoenix. Thomas Daine and his team successfully managed to stop them, but the group turned out to be linked to the Chaos Insurgency in some way.

When the Insurgency found out, they interrogated him about it. Thomas Daine gave them a bit of information so they would back off. The Chaos Insurgency figured his skills would be valuable but still wanted revenge for their stopped plans. So they dug up some information on him and murdered his family. They told a still-captive Daine that his family was killed but swore and pleaded it was not them who did it but actually the Foundation. Why? They gave him the story that his family was in possession of an anomalous artifact, and for that, the Foundation opted to kill them for it and take it from their dead hands. Thomas Daine did not completely buy it, but they also said the Foundation would likely come for him next to cover up the incident if they let him go. They were suddenly willing to forgive what he did to the them if he joined them and helped them fight the Foundation. So he did, and he did it well. He saw a few Chaos Insurgency Operations against Foundation sites and aided in the stealing of several SCPs from Foundation hands to manipulate and use for their own gain or sell to the highest bidder. These were stored at the warehouse he was stationed at the day of the raid.

Eventually, the Chaos Insurgency let their guard down. Daine began to seem less trusting, and his brain knew something was wrong with their cover story the more he worked for them. One day during guard duty, he got his hands on some archive files and found his own in a stack that was meant to be burned. But surprise surprise, the Chaos Insurgency was too incompetent to do anything right and left them sitting there for months forgotten. He skimmed through the file, finding out that after what he did to the Insurgency, they sought revenge against him and killed his family. However, seeing his potential shouldn’t be wasted, they decided to enlist him with a made-up cover story that the Foundation was responsible for their deaths. He wanted to kill everyone there at that moment but knew he was outnumbered 43:1. So he located a Foundation contact in Insurgency Espionage files and tipped them off anonymously. That is why when the Foundation came he was so quick to surrender to them. He says he cannot fully trust them. Despite them not being responsible, he has still seen things the Foundation has done and how they operate from his many operations. This is also partly because his mind was filled with brainwashing propaganda from the Insurgency during his many years there.

The Foundation gave him lightened treatment after that compared to most of the disgruntled Sigma-66 Operatives, even permitting him free-roam across sites where he is stationed off-duty as long as he was escorted by security.

Field Operations Log

Shortly after the raid and Thomas’ subsequent cooperation and remand into Foundation custody, Agents reviewed his recovered files and expertise. He was offered a position in Sigma-66, though with strict rules regarding his enlistment. Thomas was surgically implanted with a Class-IV Neural Microchip in the neck area; removal is strictly prohibited and considered near impossible, as any attempt triggers a fail-safe electrical discharge to the brain, rendering him comatose. The same consequence applies if he disobeys protocol or leaves a designated holding area, which also emits a generic radio frequency signal to summon operatives for detainment. Thomas, along with other captives, underwent rigorous training by officers from various Mobile Task Forces to ensure combat capability and recontainment proficiency. This regimen, which included training on Foundation equipment and vehicles, spanned over a year and a half.

OPERATION ID: OP-S66-24-0511 | DATE: May 11, 2024

Objective: Secure Anomalous Assets from The Serpents Hand. Estimated Casualty Rate: 80%

Outcome: SUCCESS (5 Casualties)

Action Report: The unit infiltrated the target location via an unidentified box truck driven directly into the cargo bay of The Serpent's Hand warehouse posing as a new shipment of stolen anomalous artifacts. Upon the bay doors opening, the team immediately neutralized the two hostile members attempting to unload the vehicle. In the ensuing engagement, the unit successfully took down 12 additional members, with the remaining combatants surrendering shortly after. Daine showed exemplary work during the operation, personally eliminating 6 of the hostiles. A total of 6 Anomalous Assets, along with a cache of previously stolen Foundation equipment, were successfully recovered from the site.

OPERATION ID: OP-S66-24-0822 | DATE: August 22, 2024

Objective: Recontain SCP-076-2. Estimated Casualty Rate: 95%

Outcome: MILD SUCCESS (Loss: 7 S66, 21 Foundation, 9 Nu-7)

Action Report: On August 22, 2024, SCP-076-2 breached containment and began a hostile escape attempt, killing several Foundation Personnel along the way. Due to the high casualty rate associated with this entity, Sigma-66 was sent to act as a barrier force until further reinforcements from Nu-7 could be prepped for deployment. During the engagement, Daine provided heavy fire support from the air on a mounted machine gun utilizing armor-piercing bullets. He dealt heavy damage to SCP-076-2 while Sigma-66 units on the ground fought via armored Humvees and heavy weaponry. Nu-7 arrived and secured the target before SCP-076-2 could leave the perimeter of Containment Area 25b.
